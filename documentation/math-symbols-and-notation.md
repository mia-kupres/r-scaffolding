# Math Symbols and Notation

This document collects short explanations of mathematical symbols that appear in the supplement.

## Nabla or Del: `\nabla`

The upside-down triangle symbol is called **nabla**.

```text
\nabla
```

In rendered math, `\nabla` appears as an upside-down triangle. In HTML or Markdown text, it can also be written as `&nabla;`.

It is not a Greek letter, though it is often used in mathematics, statistics, and physics. It is also commonly called **del** or the **gradient operator**.

Example:

```text
\nabla f
```

This is read as "gradient of f" or "del f."
